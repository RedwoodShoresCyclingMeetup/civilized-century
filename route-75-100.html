North Course - 75 or 100 Miles

Choose one of four routes at packet pickup – 35, 60, 75, or 100 miles

75-mile Route

is the Lil' Engine That Could route. The mind is willing to do the 100 but the body has other plans. See how you feel at the Don Edwards Visitor Center rest-stop and turn back for 75 miles or go for the gusto.

100-mile Route

is a grand view of all the Peninsula and South Bay have to offer. Riders are taken past Seal and Coyote Points and San Francisco airport to Oyster Point Marina. Then return and join the 60-mile route for a circumnavigation of the South Bay. It’s a long way and a long day but pain is temporary, pride is forever.

Expect short stretches of yummy gravel on all routes. ROUTES ARE NOW FINAL.

Printed cue sheets are provided at packet pickup.
