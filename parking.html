Directions & Parking

Directions to Event

Check-in/Packet Pick-up, Start Line, and Finish Line are at Sports Basement, 202 Walnut St, Redwood City, CA.

  • From Veterans Blvd, turn onto WALNUT STREET (NOT Main or Convention)
  • Go ALMOST to the end of Walnut Street and turn left into Kohl Plaza
  • Park in any space nearest the freeway, AWAY from Sports Basement
  • Parking is free, unattended, and at your own risk.

Drivers - watch out for your fellow cyclists! Check-in/packet pickup opens at 8 am.
