Directions & Parking

Directions to Event

Registration, Start Line, and Finish Line are at Pelican Cove Condominium Association, Redwood Shores.

  • From the north - take US101-South, exit Ralston and stay right. At light, turn left onto Marine Parkway.
  • From the south - take US101-North, exit Ralston/Marine Parkway, and turn right onto Marine Parkway.
  • Once on Marine Parkway, continue east past Oracle (iconic blue buildings).
  • At Bridge Parkway (traffic light), either
    • turn right, then IMMEDIATE right into Curion Insights - 350 Bridge Pkwy (best)
    • turn left, then IMMEDIATE left into Redwood Shores Library - 399 Marine Parkway (overflow)
  • Parking is free but unattended
  • With bike, cross the street to Pelican Cove Condominium Association.

Registration opens at 8 am.

Where to Park

The "Civilized Century" is a cycling event organized by the Redwood Shores Cycling Meetup.
