Hydration, Hydration, Hydration

"Hot enough for ya?" – be prepared for the heat:

  • Sunscreen
  • Plenty of fluids with electrolytes
  • Look for the small cans of V8
  • Look for the salty snacks
  • Take a bottle of pickle juice with you
  • Put ice in your water bottle at the rest-stops

Rider Safety

All riders must wear a helmet. If by chance you forgot yours, don’t worry. Will be able to borrow a sweaty one.

RIDERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO FOLLOW ALL TRAFFIC LAWS. BE ADVISED — RIDING WITH TWO EARBUDS IN IS AGAINST THE LAW.

The "Civilized Century" is a cycling event organized by the Redwood Shores Cycling Meetup.
