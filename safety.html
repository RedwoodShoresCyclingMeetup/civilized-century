Safety

COVID-19

Please help us be in compliance with state guidelines and provide a safe and healthy environment for all.

  • If day-of the event, you feel unwell, please pass on this event for the safety of others. A refund will be provided.
  • Inside PCCA, registrants will be limited to TBD persons and WEARING A FACE COVERING IS MANDATORY.
  • Hands may be washed inside the building. A portable bottle of hand sanitizer will be provided for each rider at registration.
  • Social distancing guidelines should be observed at all times.
  • Be aware of what and who you touch – your face, the face of others, etc.

Hydrate, Hydrate, Hydrate

"Hot enough for ya?" – be prepared for the heat:

  • Sunscreen (including scalp)
  • Plenty of fluids with electrolytes
  • Look for the small cans of V8
  • Look for the salty snacks
  • Take a bottle of pickle juice with you
  • Put ice in your water bottle at the rest-stops
  • Have on too much clothing? Drop in a bag at first three rest-stops. Mark with name and phone #. We'll bring to Clubhouse.

Rider Safety

All riders must wear a helmet. If by chance you forgot yours, don’t worry. Will be able to borrow a sweaty one.

RIDERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO FOLLOW ALL TRAFFIC LAWS. BE ADVISED — RIDING WITH TWO EARBUDS IN IS AGAINST THE LAW.

The "Civilized Century" is a cycling event organized by the Redwood Shores Cycling Meetup.
