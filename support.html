Support

Rider Support

Event is fully-ish supported by volunteers. Routes will be marked in addition to cue sheets. Restroom facilities at every stop. Five rest-stops lightly stocked but count on water and Gatorade, fruit, and energy bars. HIGHLY RECOMMENDED TO HAVE BREAKFAST BEFORE THE EVENT.

Email to volunteer.

SAG Support

SAG vehicle will be sweeping the course. Call (650) 000-0000 for road side assistance.

Hydrate, Hydrate, Hydrate

"Hot enough for ya?" – be prepared for the heat:

  • Sunscreen (including scalp)
  • Plenty of fluids with electrolytes
  • Look for the small cans of V8
  • Look for the salty snacks
  • Put ice in your water bottle at the rest-stops
  • Have on too much clothing? Drop in a bag at first or second rest-stop. Mark with bib and phone #. Pickup clothing and T-shirts at Oracle stop postride.

Rider Safety

All riders must wear a helmet. If by chance you forgot yours, don’t worry. Will be able to borrow a sweaty one.

RIDERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO FOLLOW ALL TRAFFIC LAWS. BE ADVISED — RIDING WITH TWO EARBUDS IN IS AGAINST THE LAW.

The "Civilized Century" is a cycling event organized by the Redwood Shores Cycling Meetup.
