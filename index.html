The Ride

Want to enjoy the pinnacle of cycling goals but without the hassles. Perhaps the Civilized Century is for you! A "Century" is 100 miles.

  • No ugly start times. Ride out at 9am or later!
  • No hills, hills, and more hills. It's pretty much flat-ish!
  • No time gates. There's 14 hours of daylight but 10 ought to do it.
  • No long, early morning drive or expensive hotel stay. It's local!
  • No rain. It never rains in California or so the song says.
  • No wind. Well that, we cannot promise.
  • No huge registration fee. It's $30.

The course offers scenic views of the Bay Area, a jaunt across Dumbarton Bridge, loop around Facebook campus, and a few hidden trails. Designed for safety, the South Bay section is clockwise to minimize left turns. And an overall portion of the course is trail, bike lane, or low car traffic.
